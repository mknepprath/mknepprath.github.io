Christmas Cards and Holiday Photo Cards Make It A Holiday to Remember

Pear Tree Greetings' award-winning Christmas cards and holiday photo cards, with their standout designs and countless ways to personalize, make it easy to create a holiday to remember. Our holiday collection includes everything from affordable two-sided cards in the latest colors and trends, to religious Christmas cards with a fresh twist, to our most innovative Golden Pear Christmas cards that include multiple folds, real foil stamped Christmas cards, photo cards that stand up like picture frames, folded cards tied with ribbons, pop up cards, spinner cards and more.


Surprisingly affordable and easy to personalize with photos, text and over 70 color choices, our unique designs let you showcase your amazing style, while our easy-to-use website helps you give them a look that's all your own. Most of our holiday photo cards are printed on our signature, 100 lb. matte-finish card stock, made from 100 percent, post-consumer recycled paper. This premium quality, eco-friendly card stock has been carefully chosen to give your cards a high-quality feel at an affordable price. Our affordable photo paper Christmas cards are a notable exception, which are printed on professional photo paper with a semi-glossy Lustre Finish.


You'll also find all the little extras you need to make the holiday special at Pear Tree: Christmas address labels to match your Christmas cards, Christmas gift tags and stickers for personalized wrapping, and Christmas thank you cards to show your appreciation. And our Christmas mini note cards make perfect hostess gifts, or gift card enclosures for your mailed gifts. Shop our entire holiday collection for morme ways to make your holiday the best ever!


Celebrating a new baby? You'll want to browse our collection of Christmas birth announcements. And if you're planning a party, our holiday party invitations, for both Christmas and New Year's Eve, with matching holiday party decorations are sure to add sparkle and shine to the event. Easily customizable with colors and editable text, you can set the mood for your party in minutes.


You'll also love our unique collection of personalized gifts for that someone special. Turn your favorite photos into favorite gifts, including custom phone cases, personalized ornaments, wall art, wall decals or custom posters for your teen's bedroom. And our kids' coloring books, custom notepads and personalized note cards make great stocking stuffers.


With most of our signature card stock items taking just 1-2 days to print, we can have your holiday order shipped and on the way to your mailbox in no time. It's easy to see why the holidays are merrier when you plan them at Pear Tree Greetings!
