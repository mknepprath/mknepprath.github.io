    APO/FPO Military Address

    To ship to a United States Military Address:


    1. Enter the APO or FPO code into the City field.

    2. In the State/Province field, select:

      AA ( Armed Forces of the Americas )
      The Americas, excluding Canada
      (Zip codes beginning with 340)

      AE ( Armed Forces Europe )
      Europe, Middle East. Africa. Canada
      (Zip codes beginning with 340)

      AP ( Armed Forces Pacific )
      Pacific
      (Zip codes beginning with 962 through 966)

    3. In the ZIP/Postal Code field, enter the appropriate ZIP code.

      Examples:
      • FPO AE 09508
      • APO AE 09815
      • APO AP 96207
      • FPO AA 34091
      • FPO AA 96522
      • APO AA 34030
