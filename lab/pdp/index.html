Birth Announcements -- My Baby Girl Ribbon Booklet

Item Number PTG33962G2

A white ribbon is threaded through these unique birth announcements filled with photos of your precious new baby girl, then folded up and tied with a bow for friends and family to open. The front panel features one vertical photo, with your choice of three welcoming messages: 'Our New Arrival,' 'Hello World,' or 'Say Hello To....' You can change the color of the message and add her name below it to make sure it stands out on your photo. You can also change the color of the binding edge. Inside, the first two panels hold your baby girl's full name, and another full photo. When these two panels are opened, four photos form a collage in the center, with all the birth details on one side and room for a personal message, story, quote or blessing on the right. Your baby girl's initials are featured in a color block in the center. All the text inside is editable and all the solid colors can be changed. These unique birth announcements are sure to become a keepsake for close friends and family.
